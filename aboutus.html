Apple is a prominent hardware and software company best known for its series of smart personal laptops, phones, tablets, watches, and headphones

Apple was founded as Apple Computer Company on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne to develop and sell Wozniak's Apple I personal computer.

Apple's main purpose is to create products that enrich people's daily lives.

Apple's brand value had astronomically ascended due to its relentless focus on keeping its customers happy with its well designed hardware backed up by its proprietary software.
